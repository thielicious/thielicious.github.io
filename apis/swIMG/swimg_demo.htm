Image SRC Extraction



This is a standard pattern, which simply extracts the image source out of the thumbnails and displays them in fullview.



				
  <script src="apis/swIMG/swIMG.js"></script>
  <script>swIMG();</script>

  <div class="swIMG">	
    <img src="./demos/swIMG/_img/small/small_e.jpg" class="sw_image">
    <img src="./demos/swIMG/_img/small/small_x.jpg" class="sw_image">
    <img src="./demos/swIMG/_img/small/small_a.jpg" class="sw_image">
    <img src="./demos/swIMG/_img/small/small_m.jpg" class="sw_image">
    <img src="./demos/swIMG/_img/small/small_p.jpg" class="sw_image">
  </div>
